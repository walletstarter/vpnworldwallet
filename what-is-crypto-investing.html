What is Crypto Investing?

Crypto investing involves allocating capital to digital assets such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins with the goal of long-term growth or short-term profit. Unlike traditional investing, crypto markets operate 24/7 and are driven by different economic, social, and technological factors.

Key Concepts

  • Blockchain: The foundational technology that powers cryptocurrencies
  • Volatility: Prices can swing dramatically, offering both opportunity and risk
  • Wallets: Tools to securely store crypto assets (hot vs cold storage)
  • Exchanges: Platforms like Gemini, Coinbase, and Binance to buy/sell crypto

Benefits and Risks

Crypto investing can offer high returns but comes with unique risks including regulatory uncertainty, custodial security, and market manipulation. Diversification and research are essential for long-term success.
