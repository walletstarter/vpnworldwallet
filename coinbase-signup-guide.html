How to Get Your Coinbase Bonus

Coinbase offers sign-up incentives when you complete verification and make a qualifying deposit. It’s one of the fastest U.S.-based ways to enter crypto, especially for beginners or fiat-native investors.

Why Coinbase?

Coinbase is a publicly traded, U.S.-regulated crypto platform with FDIC-insured USD balances, a clean UX, mobile-first trading, and an NFT & DeFi gateway through Coinbase Wallet. It’s beginner-friendly with instant buy/sell tools, but also offers advanced tools like Coinbase Pro for power users.

Steps to Claim Your Bonus

  1. Go to coinbase.com and click “Get Started.”
  2. Enter your name, email, and a strong password.
  3. Complete ID verification with a photo ID (KYC).
  4. Link a payment method (bank, card, or PayPal).
  5. Buy or deposit $100+ in crypto to unlock your bonus.

Tips for Fast Approval
